Europe Trip 2019: Italy, Switzerland & France

"Wanderlust: (n.) a strong desire for or impulse to wander or travel and explore the world"

Italy

The land where pasta was birthed. Had pasta every single day for every meal and I still couldn't get enough of it! One thing that I learned from that trip was to not make any plans on public holiday. The only way you could get around was ON FOOT. We travelled from Rome to Florence before ending the Italy trip at Venice.

Switzerland

We took a 6-hour high speed train from Venice to Switzerland, Interlaken. Over there, we were greeted with the Swiss alps and snow-capped mountains every morning, and visited the Top of Europe - which was simply breathtaking and intensely cold. Not only was the food there expensive, it only tasted mediocre, and we resorted to korean instant noodles for $5 Singapore Dollars from an Asian supermarket. We also had our first attempt at skiing with the alps surrounding us, which was not a very pleasant experience as we fell more than we skiied, but the sights ultimately compensated for that.

France

Bonjour Monsieur/Mademoiselle! Comment-allez vous? As this was our last stop of our Europe tour, we were already very exhausted from all the packing and moving about. We decided that we needed a comfortable place to stay at Paris and be surrounded by good food. One funny thing is that instead of going for the local cuisine, we ended up eating asian cuisine for almost every meal. The only local thing that we liked is probably the croissants.
